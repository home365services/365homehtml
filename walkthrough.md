# 365home.in - Platform Walkthrough

Welcome to **365home.in**, a state-of-the-art service marketplace designed for modern households.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Multi-Category Service Grid
We've implemented all **17 requested services** across 4 logical groups:
- **Technical**: AC Repair, Electrician, Plumber, CCTV, etc.
- **Home Improvement**: Civil Works, Painting, Carpenter, etc.
- **Lifestyle & Care**: Maid Service, Cleaning, Catering.
- **Insta-Help**: High-priority emergency repairs.

### 2. Intelligent Booking System
The platform distinguishes between standard repairs and high-ticket projects:
- **Book Now**: For standard services like AC Repair or Plumbing, you get a real-time calendar picker.
- **Get a Quote**: For Civil Works and Building Plans, the UI automatically switches to a comprehensive quote request form to capture lead details.

### 3. ⚡ Insta-Help (Emergency Mode)
A floating **"Insta-Help"** button is persistent on every page. Clicking it immediately redirects the user to the emergency service booking with a priority status flag.

### 4. 🛠️ Admin Dashboard
Designed for service providers and administrators to manage operations.
- **Access**: Click "Provider Login" in the navbar and enter pin `365` (experimental).
- **Functionality**: View recent bookings, revenue metrics, and dispatch technicians for "Urgent" requests.

### 5. 📚 Buying Tips (Blog)
A dedicated resource section where users can find articles on where to buy the cheapest home appliances and materials, helping them save money beyond just services.

## 🎨 Visual Identity
- **Palette**: Professional Blue (#0056b3) and Clean White (#ffffff) to instill trust and reliability.
- **Aesthetics**: Glassmorphism navigation, smooth hover animations, and high-quality generated imagery.

## 📱 Mobile Experience
The site is fully responsive. On mobile:
- The navigation collapses into a hamburger menu.
- The Insta-Help button becomes a compact floating icon for quick thumb access.
